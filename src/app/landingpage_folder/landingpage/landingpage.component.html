What Is A Goaltracker?

  • A Goaltracker is a system where you write your goals down to track them
  • The process of writing goals down helps you to remember and to commit to your goals
  • If you achieve a goal and check it off, your brain releases dopamine which gives you the desire to accomplish more goals
  • Therefore a goaltracker makes you commit to a goal and at the same time gives you a desire to accomplish more goals
